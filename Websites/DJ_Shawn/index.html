About DJ Shawn
" With over 20 years of experience, DJ, Entrepreneur, Promoter, Businessman and Music Enthusiast DJ Shawn hails out of Miami, FL, with over 100,000 songs in all musical genres, ready to rock the party.

Having spent many years playing at all of the major local night clubs (alongside local hard-hitters and national acts alike), holding multiple residencies, DJing special events and promoting his own shows, DJ Shawn has had a lot of time to develop his sound.

For him, it’s all about mood and a deep, hypnotic groove… playing those tracks that get you tapping your feet and nodding your head without you realizing it, regardless of genre, tempo, style, or release date. Don’t be surprised when you suddenly find yourself dancing. "
